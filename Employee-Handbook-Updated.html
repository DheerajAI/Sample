Employee Handbook FAQs
GreytHR
1. What is GreytHR?
GreytHR is an employees Self-Service Portal where the employees can view their salary
slips, update income tax declaration, submit income tax proof, view form 16, get salary-to-
date summary and TDS details.
2. How can I get set up on GreytHR?
New Employees will be receiving an email with a link to create login credentials after 5th of the
next month of joining.
Example if an employee joined on January 1st the link will be sent after 5th February.
The access will be provided upon processing of the1st months salary and post generation of
payslip for the said employee.
3. I have lost my password, how do I log-in again?
Employees can click on FORGOT PASSWORD on the login page of GreytHR.
An email link will be sent to your official email account to reset the password.
4. GreytHR - I have tried to reset the password but nothing appears on the email for
resetting the same. How should I proceed?
If the employees are facing such issues, then they can send an email to accounts@valenta.io
we will re-generate the login credentials.
5. Where can I find my employee ID for GreytHR?
The HR team will share the employee ID with Zoho Credentials via email on the date of
joining.
6. When are payslips made available each month?
Payslips will be available after 20th of next month. Example Salary is processed for the month
January, the Jan month payslips will be available after 20th February. An email intimation will
be sent to employees once the payslips are published.
7. How can I download my historical payslips?
Historical payslips can be downloaded in GreytHR employee login. Once the employee logins
to the portal. The employee needs to click on SALARY tab then click on PAYSLIPS- then
select the month on top right corner. And click on the download symbol on the top right
corner.
8. As a trainee, will I receive a payslip via GreytHR as well?
No, trainees do-not receive any GreytHR logins & payslips.
9. Does a payslip get generated while on long leave due to medical / any other reasons?
Yes, the payslips will be generated during long leave. If there are no earnings for the period,
then the payslip shall show zero.
10. How can I submit my IT declaration?
IT declaration can be submitted from April to December. The declaration window will be
opened on the 21st of each month & closed on the 30/31st of the same month.
The employees have 10 days time for updating the IT declaration.
Once the employee logins to the portal. The employee needs to click on SALARY tab then
click on IT DECLARATIONS. If the declaration window is enabled its shows OPEN, then
the employee needs to click on START NOW, enter all the declarations & submit. If the
window is closed it shows LOCKED.
If the declaration window is enabled an email intimation will be sent to the employee.
11. How many times can IT declaration be updated in one year?
There is no restriction in updating IT declaration. The employees can update on a monthly
basis between 21st to 30/31stof same month.
12. When does the window to submit proof of investment open?
The submission of investment proof will be enabled in the month of January during the
financial year. And 15 days will be given to employees to upload the proofs. An email
intimation will be sent once the window is enabled.
Once the employee logins to the portal. The employee needs to click on SALARY tab then
click on PROOF OF INVESTMENTS.
13. I was on leave during the window to submit Proof of Investments (POI) and missed the
deadline, what can I do next?
15 days time will be provided for the employees to update. If the employee is on leave all 15
days, then the employee needs to inform the HR department regarding this. We will enable
the window again.
14. When will my Form-16 be made available in GreytHR?
Form 16 will be made available in the month of June after the end of the financial year.
15. Can my form-16 be provided to me on priority basis upon request?
No, Form16 can be generated in the month of June only, it cant be issued across the year.
16. Who can I contact for any further questions related to access to GreytHR, Payslip, IT
declaration or procuring Form 16?
If the employees have any issues regarding this, then they can send an email to
accounts@valenta.io addressing the query.
Employees should mention their Employee ID in the email.
This is applicable for employees who have resigned from the organization as well.
Salary Structure -
1. There is a difference in my CTC and salary in hand, what is the breakup of my salary?
CTC means Cost to Company which covers all the benefits paid to employees by the company.
It includes Gross salary, Employer PF and Gratuity. Gross salary includes all salary
components like Basic, HRA, conveyance, medical, & special allowances. But it excludes
Employer PF & Gratuity. Salary in Hand means the amount, which is received after deducting
Employee PT, Group Medical Insurance, Professional Tax, TDS (Income tax) from gross salary.
Salary in Hand will vary from employee to employee based on their Income tax and IT
declarations made for the year
2. What is the difference between the new and old tax regime?
Under the old tax regime, the employee can show all the deductions u/s 80C, 80D, 80G etc.
Employees can claim allowances like HRA with higher income tax slab rate. But under the
new tax regime the above deductions are not available, but the income tax slab rate is less.
The employee should compare their tax liability with both the regime. Based on the tax
benefits the employee needs to select either the old or new tax regime. The tax slabs may
vary as per notification by Government during yearly budget announcements
3. How is the statutory deduction in my salary structured?
There are four types of deductions
a. Employee Provident Fund - will be a fixed deduction of Rs. 1,800.
b. Professional Tax - will be a fixed deduction of Rs. 200
c. Group Medical Insurance (GMI)
d. Tax deducted at source (TDS or Income Tax)

4. How is gratuity collected and paid?
Gratuity is collected from the CTC of every employee. It is applicable to the employees who
have worked for a minimum term of 5 years. When the employee resigns after working for 5
years, then the employee is entitled to receive gratuity. If the employee resigns without
completing 5 years, then no gratuity will be paid.

1. Will my salary structure change after Increment?

2. My PF contribution is not reflecting in my EPF Fund, what is the reason for this?

grievance with PF department on https://epfigms.gov.in/
3. How can I get set-up for with Provident Fund with my bank account?
a. Employees can login to their UAN portal www.unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in,
under manage tab, select KYC option from drop down menu.
b. Select Documents, enter bank account number and IFSC and click save.After
saving new bank details, it will show KYC pending for approval.
c. Submit Documents to Employer.
d. After documents verification by Employer, KYC pending for approval will change to
Digitally Approved KYC.
e. Employees will receive a confirmation message from EPFO, after successful
verification of bank details by employer.
